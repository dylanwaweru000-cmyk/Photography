About the Photographer

I am a self-taught photographer based in Kenya, drawn to the small, everyday moments that often go unnoticed
The way light falls on a street, a stranger's expression, the quiet in-between instants.
I started out with nothing more than curiosity and a camera phone, and over time photography became my way of paying closer attention to the world around me.
This gallery is a collection of images I've captured while learning to see differently — each one a small story worth telling
